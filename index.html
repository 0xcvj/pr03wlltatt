From
Idea To Ink

Here's how we bring your tattoo to life — guided every step of the way. From start to finish, we ensure your vision becomes a stunning reality.

01

Share Your Idea

Start by sending us a conversation. Tell us your vision — references, placement, size. We'll discuss your ideas, answer questions, and set a consultation date that works perfectly for you.

02

Bringing It to Paper

Once I understand your vision, I create a unique sketch tailored to your style. We'll review it together — make adjustments if needed — until it's exactly what you imagined, then beyond.

03

The Tattoo Session

Settle in, put on some good music, and let me work through the piece. Comfort is paramount. Breaks when you need them. We'll take as long as it takes to get it perfect.

04

Keeping It Sharp

Good aftercare is as important as the session itself. You'll receive clear instructions and I'm always available for questions during healing. A touch-up is included if needed.
